Top 3 Movies of All Time According to Mark

My top 3 movies of all-time

Life of Pi

Whenever someone asks me what my favorite movie is, there is not a single time that I didn't think of Life of Pi. The story revolves around 2 characters, a kid and a lion, who are stuck together in a lifeboat. With the threat of a big lion just waiting to eat the boy alive, Pi must find a way to survive. Admittedly, I don't remember much from this movie, and there is a possibility that I've enjoyed other movies way more than this. But there is no denying the impact this movie had on me, seeing a kid survive on his own with an apex predator. Especially since I was only around 8 years old back then. I can't help but relate to the character.

Avengers: Endgame

As a Marvel's fan, you should have seen this one coming. Avengers: Endgame (and Avengers, Infinity War) are undoutedly peak Marvel. I just love how Marvel was able to bring all these previously unrelated characters together to fight against a common greater enemy. The cinematography and writing for this movie was phenomenal and you can see that the movie staff really poured their hearts out on this one. I daresay that there might be no other Marvel movie that can top this one off. Definitely worth the hype it garnered and will always live up to our expectation.

Maquia: When the Promised Flower Blooms

This one, I don't remember much as well. But I have no regrets watching it. I can't say that it's the best anime movie out there, but it definitely holds a very strong and large place in my heart. Watching this movie while my mother was sleeping beside me made my bawl my eyes out. The way Maquia dedicated her life to taking care of a boy and the hardships she went through as a single mother to provide and care for her unbiological son made me feel things which made me cry like a mad baby. Would love to watch this again when I have the time.
